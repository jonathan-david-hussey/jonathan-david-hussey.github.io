*London, UK · Remote · Full-time · Aug 2019 – May 2024*

- Ran a performance programme on our Elasticsearch cluster after months of degrading search: 20+ targeted fixes that cut average latency 89%, ended threadpool rejections almost entirely, and brought flagship infrastructure costs down 92%.
- The platform suffered site-wide outages several times a day. Redesigned the worst pipelines around producer/consumer queues and proper concurrency, downtime went to zero, and the company awarded shares for it.
- Found and fixed a five-year-old defect in the core Twitter ingestion pipeline that had silently capped data yield at 6.25% per request. Restoring it to 100% lifted search, reporting and profile quality across every product.
- Led the Twitter API v1-to-v2 migration across ten dependent applications, dependency analysis, rate-limit redesign, phased rollout, without downstream disruption.
- Built and deployed an end-to-end tweet classifier handling 125M records a month, owning everything from feature engineering and model selection through deployment and monitoring.
- Five years of code review across 30 repositories, 500+ reviews, alongside building ingestion pipelines for YouTube and podcast data on AWS with capacity to scale 100x.
